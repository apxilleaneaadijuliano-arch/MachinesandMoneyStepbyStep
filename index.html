⚙️ Multi‑use of Machines 💖💰

Group 5

A creative solution to the high cost of machines ✨

Step 1: Group Discussion 💬

Our group revisited the problem from our previous RCA: the high cost of machines. We brainstormed several possible solutions, such as sharing equipment, repairing old machines, or finding cheaper alternatives. However, most of these ideas had limitations. After weighing the pros and cons, we realized that focusing on multi‑use food machines could directly address the issue by combining functions into one affordable device.

Step 2: Research 📚🥤🍳

We researched common household food machines and explored how they could be adapted for multiple uses:

  • 🌀 Blender – smoothies, soups, grinding spices
  • 🍲 Steamer – vegetables, dumplings, fish
  • 🔪 Chopping Tool – vegetables, fruits, nuts
  • 🍞 Mixer – dough, whipping cream, pasta making

Step 3: Concluding Ideas ✨

From our research, we concluded that multi‑use machines are practical because they:

  • 💰 Reduce the need to buy separate appliances (lowering costs)
  • 🏠 Save space in kitchens or classrooms
  • 🌱 Encourage sustainability by extending the usefulness of existing machines

Step 4: Prototype Concept 🍚

Our prototype demonstrates how one machine can perform multiple tasks. For example, a rice cooker prototype could:

  • 🍚 Cook rice (main function)
  • 🥟 Steam vegetables and dumplings
  • 🍲 Slow‑cook soups or stews
  • 🍰 Bake simple cakes or bread
